# Persona: Competitive Player - Marcus

![Marcus's Avatar](marcus-avatar.png)

## Demographics
- **Name:** Marcus Chen
- **Age:** 24
- **Occupation:** Software Engineering Student
- **Tech Savviness:** High
- **Gaming Experience:** Experienced with strategy games

## Background
Marcus is a computer science student who enjoys strategic thinking and competitive gameplay. He plays Connect4 with his friends during study breaks and takes pride in developing winning strategies. He appreciates well-designed interfaces and notices details in game design.

## Goals & Motivations
- **Strategic Challenge:** Wants to analyze moves and plan ahead
- **Competition:** Enjoys trying to outsmart opponents
- **Skill Development:** Likes to improve his gameplay strategy over time
- **Social Gaming:** Uses games as social activities with friends
- **Quality Experience:** Appreciates polished, well-designed applications

## Pain Points
- Frustrated by laggy or unresponsive touch controls
- Annoyed by unclear game state or ambiguous rules
- Dislikes when he can't see the full board clearly
- Wants to know if someone has won immediately, not after a delay
- Bothered by poor visual design or inconsistent UI

## User Needs
- **Responsive Touch Controls:** Needs instant feedback when interacting with the game
- **Clear Game State:** Wants to see all possible moves and the current board state clearly
- **Win Detection:** Expects immediate and accurate win/tie detection
- **Move Preview:** Would appreciate seeing where the piece will land before confirming
- **Game History:** Wants to see how many games each player has won in the current session
- **Undo/Restart:** Needs quick options to restart or undo accidental moves

## Typical Usage Scenario
Marcus and his roommate are taking a study break. They grab a tablet and want to play a best-of-5 Connect4 tournament. He expects:
1. Fast app launch with clean interface
2. Ability to see column preview when hovering/touching
3. Smooth animations when dropping pieces
4. Immediate win detection with clear highlighting of the winning line
5. Score tracking across multiple games
6. Quick restart without confirmation dialogs

## Behavioral Traits
- **Usage Pattern:** Moderate-length sessions (15-30 minutes), multiple games in a row
- **Device:** Comfortable with both portrait and landscape orientations
- **Environment:** Primarily plays indoors with good lighting
- **Social:** Enjoys competitive banter and tournament-style play
- **Feedback:** Will provide feedback or suggestions for improvement

## Design Implications
- Ultra-responsive touch controls (< 50ms response time)
- Column highlighting on hover/touch to show preview
- Smooth, satisfying animations (300-400ms for piece drops)
- Winning line highlighting with celebration animation
- Session score counter prominently displayed
- Quick restart button always accessible
- Undo button for accidental taps
- Support for both landscape and portrait orientations
- Professional, modern visual design with attention to detail
